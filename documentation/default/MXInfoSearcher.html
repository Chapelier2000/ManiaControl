Searches MX for maps matching name, author and/or environment;  or search MX for the 10 most recent maps

	
    
				
  • return: If ->valid() is false, no matching map was found;         otherwise, an iterator of MXInfo objects for a 'foreach' loop.         Returns at most 100 maps ($maxpage * 20).
